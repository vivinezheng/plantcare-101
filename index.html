Plant Care 101

Here you can find information and helpful tips on many common "easy" houseplants. Though succulents and similar low-care plants are often advertised as starter plants, they need a few things that beginner horticulturists might not know about in order to thrive. Check out our links to see if you are giving your plants the care they deserve.

Epiphytes

If you have ever seen an "air plant" in the store, own an epiphyte, or are just curious about these cool plants, make sure to check out this page. Epiphytes can be some of the easiest plants to care for, if you follow our tips on keeping them happy.

Succulents

Succulents are some of the most common houseplants—and for good reason. These lovable autotrophs come in tons of colors, shapes, and sizes, so there's one for everyone. Visit this page to see if you can find the one for you.
